How the Tiers Work

Tier 1 — Deal-breakers

Binary pass/fail criteria. A property must meet all of these or it's out:

  • Budget ≤$2.2M
  • Step-free access / lift
  • Transport ≤1.5km
  • Daily supplies walkable
  • In target area
  • Lawful residential zoning

Tier 2 — Scoring discriminators

For properties that pass Tier 1, these rank them by quality:

  • Outlook (leading) — water, park, or elevated views
  • Living area ≥115 m²
  • Light and aspect
  • Warehouse character
  • Nearby pool, parks, restaurants

The "Tier 1 pass only" checkbox filters to show only viable options. The Tier 2 score helps you choose between them.
